Banh Bao
Ingredients:
  • 12 grams dried woodear mushroom
  • 12 oz ground pork
  • 1 teaspoon granulated cane sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 green onions (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup finely diced yellow onion
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas and carrots
  • 2 oz Chinese sausage (finely diced)
  • 12 hard-boiled quail eggs
  • 1 bag TL Bon Con Voi Corp banh bao premix
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated cane sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar (optional)
  • 12 cupcake liners
Instructions:
  1. Hydrate dried wood ear mushroom in hot water for 10 minutes, then mince.
  2. Prepare the dough by mixing TL Bon Con Voi Corp banh bao flour mix, milk, sugar, and oil, then knead until smooth and set aside.
  3. Combine pork, mushrooms, onions, peas, carrots, sausage, and seasonings to make the filling.
  4. Shape filling into balls and insert hard-boiled quail eggs in the center.
  5. Steam the filling balls for 5 minutes to remove moisture, then wrap in dough and pleat.
  6. Steam the baos for 10-12 minutes until cooked and golden brown.
