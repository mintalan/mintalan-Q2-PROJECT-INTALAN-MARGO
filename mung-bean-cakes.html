Mung Bean Cakes
Ingredients:
  • 3/4 cup (155g) split mung beans (moong dal)
  • 1 to 1 1/4 cups (225g) red bean paste
  • 7 tablespoons (84g) sugar (use 6 tablespoons for less sweet cakes)
  • 3 tablespoons (45g) unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1 tablespoon virgin or unrefined coconut oil
Instructions (Summary):
  1. Rinse mung beans and soak overnight.
  2. Steam beans for 25 minutes, then add sugar and butter. Steam for another 5-7 minutes.
  3. Blend steamed beans with coconut oil into a smooth paste while still hot.
  4. Form mung bean balls, shape them into shallow discs, and wrap each around a red bean paste ball.
  5. Use a mooncake press to shape the mung bean cakes.
  6. Store the cakes at room temperature for a few hours or refrigerate for up to 3-4 days.
