Coconut Ice Cream
Ingredients:
  • 2 cans full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)
Instructions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and let the mixture cool to room temperature.
  3. Once cooled, pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and follow the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  5. Before serving, top with shredded coconut if desired. Serve and enjoy!
