Num Pang
Ingredients:
  • 1 demi-baguette
  • Sriracha mayonnaise
  • 4-5 pieces of turkey (or chicken)
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 12 small sprigs of coriander
  • 12 basil leaves
  • 12 mint leaves
  • Pickled carrots and daikon
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (355°F).
  2. Warm the demi-baguette in the oven for about a minute.
  3. Slice the baguette lengthwise without cutting through completely.
  4. Spread Sriracha mayonnaise on both halves.
  5. Layer coriander, cucumber slices, basil, turkey, and mint.
  6. Add pickled carrots and daikon, plus extra herbs for garnish.
  7. Drizzle with more Sriracha for added spice, if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
